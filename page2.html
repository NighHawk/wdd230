Principles of Design

Contrast

Pullman Parks and Rec

Pullman Parks and Rec url

I really like how the way they use contrast of both colors and fonts. With the colors, we see how they have the initial background as being white, but they also use several other eye popping colors to make it look more interesting. The same thing with the fonts. We see some are plain but others are bolded or even different colors. Both of these help the site catch our eyes and make it look more interesting.

Alignment

Espn

Espn

With the ESPN website, as I look at the home page as well as most of the other pages. I see that they have everything aligned straight down the center. With this it's easy to see where everything is and while I read the different sections, I am not constantly looking around the screen trying to find what I am looking for. Everything is easy to read and find while keeping everything aligned right down the center. On the other hand, they have a lot of blank white space on the sides. This to me is a little distracting and ugly.

Proximity

Google

Google

What I like about Googles homepage is that when you arrive, the main image and search area are right at eye level and in the upper middle of the screen. This is nice and well done with seeing that each piece is in an easy to spot location where you aren't looking around the entire screen trying to find where to type in your search. To me, I feel like its classy in the sense that there is a lot there but nothing really at all. The image and search bar are big enough to fill space but there isn't a ton of random stuff on there to distract from the use of the site.
