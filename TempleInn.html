Find Rest From Your Labours

Welcome to the Temple Inn & Suites. Here we pride ourselves in helping those whom serve in the House of Our Lord. We are located in all major cities in the United States. Here, we offer a nice relaxing place for full time missionaries as well as those who are desiring to stay a little longer while serving or attending the Temple. Temple Spotlight

The Spokane Washington Temple is located in the heart of the Spokane Valley. Built in a valley surrounded by mountains.

The Spokane Washington Temple was dedicated in the year 1999 by President Gordon B. Hinkley. It is the 59th dedicated temple in operation.
